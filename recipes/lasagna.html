Vegetable Lasagna

This hearty, vegetable lasagna is the only lasagna I will ever eat. I absolutely love it! Hope you all enjoy it as much as I do.

Ingredients for this wonderful dish

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound fresh mushroom
  • 3/4 cup of chopped onion
  • 3 gloves of garlic
  • 4 mozzarella cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil

Recipe instructions

  1. cook lasagna in large pot for 10 minutes
  2. heat oil in large saucepan, add the dry ingredients, stir for 5 minutes.
  3. mix ricotta and 2 cup of mozzarella cheese together and eggs
  4. preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  5. layer the ingredients in the baking dish
  6. bake for 40 minutes, uncovered,when finish, rest for 15 minutes
