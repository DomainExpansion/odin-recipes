favourite adobo

Introduction

a classic filipino dish, a chicken stew marinated in spice and soy sauce. Every filipino household have their own version of adobo so we cannot really tell which one is the best amongst them, but one thing is certain, they are all good

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of chicken
  • 3 pieces of dried bay leaves
  • 8 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • vinegar
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of peppercorn

Instruction

  1. marinate chicken with soy sauce, garlic, pepper for one hour
  2. heat oil in a cooking pot
  3. panfry marinated chicken for two minutes
  4. pour the marinade sauce including garli and add water, bring to a boil
  5. add dry bay leaves and whole peppercorn. simmer for 30 minutes
  6. add vinegar and stir and cook for 10 minutes
  7. put the sugar and salt to your liking and share adn enjoy
